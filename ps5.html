playstation 5


The PS5 was developed by Sony and announced in 2019. Being the most recent console, it is the most powerful and modern. It features Ultra HD Blu-Ray Discs and 4k resolution. The PS5 was released on November 12, 2020, in Australia, Japan, New Zealand, North America, and South Korea. Worldwide release followed shortly after a week. The PS5 is part of the ninth generation of consoles, including the “Xbox Series X” and “Series S” consoles, released both in the same month. The PS5 ’s controller is the farthest controller from the original one, with a sleek, modern white design. It features triggers with force feedback, which provides resistance to the player. A built in microphone button was added, meaning online gamers could communicate using only their controllers. The PS5 was greatly praised at launch due to its sleek, modern design and responsive controllers. Not only is the PlayStation 5 very powerful and useful, but it is also aesthetically pleasing- making it possibly the best one yet.
